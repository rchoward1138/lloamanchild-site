KITTY KIDS

✨ CLICK ANY PHOTO TO READ MORE ✨

Garfield

The orange fluff philosopher. Believes all problems can be solved with naps and passive judgment. His meow is oddly delicate for a cat who looks like he could bench press a ferret.

Snowy

The flame-point sweetheart with eyes big enough to guilt you into sharing dinner. Once lost his voice as a kitten under a couch, then returned meowing like he'd unlocked a new superpower.

Punkin'

The bold explorer who hates thunderstorms and loves head pats only on his schedule. He's pretty sure he's in charge of the porch, the yard, and possibly all of Arkansas.

Daisy

The matriarch. Small, scrappy, and wise, with a left eye that's seen too much nonsense. She looks gentle, but she runs the feline underground with quiet authority.

Camo

The tortie mom and self-appointed hunter. Has a stare that says, "I could take you down, but I'll let you pet me instead." Proud mother of the four inside cats.

Tom

The social grandpa of the group. Walks around meowing like a barking seal and demands head bumps as payment for his company.

Loki

The skittish ghost cat. Once traumatized by a vet visit, now rules the shadows like a furry ninja. Appears only for treats or profound spiritual moments.

Stormy

Sweet, gray-striped cuddlebug with a heart of gold. Loves belly rubs and doesn't understand personal space—proof that pure souls exist.

Zorro

The dark-furred gentleman thief. Big, social, and slightly plump from his nightly warm-cream ritual. He thinks he's mysterious, but he's mostly just spoiled.
