Life Lessons of a Man-Child

A Memoir?
by R.C. Howard
Every story has a beginning. For R.C. Howard, it began with eight dusty, forgotten tales—stories he wrote when life was simpler, dreams were bigger, and the world was filled with pure imagination, not digital domination.

What happens when those stories call out to him decades later, begging to be revisited, aching to be rewritten and demanding to be shared... who is he to say no to something unexpected?

Life Lessons of a Man-Child takes you back to where it all started, as Howard returns to his childhood, his love for Star Wars, his passion for writing, and the moments that shaped him. What he discovers isn't just nostalgia—it's a reminder of what matters most: to be creative, to be fearless, to be foolish enough to believe.
